Features of Jet Bot

Moderation

Our bot has plenty of moderation features, some commands are: ban, mute, kick and, lock just to name a few. Our goal is to have as many features that a server could need into one bot, hence our wide variation of commands!

Fun

Recently, our developers have been putting lots of time into our fun command to maximize your experience when using discord! some commands include: meme, ascii, sus, and so many more to choose from, We are constantly developing and adding more!

Utility

Jet Bot may seem like just a bot with some fun commands from the outside, but truthfully it is quite useful! We have plenty of misc/useful commands like: polls, giveaways, avatar commands, user-info and much, much more!
